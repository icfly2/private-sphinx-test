calc_da_contract_obligations(contract='amp', list_of_contracts=False, **kwargs)[source]¶

Calculate the run schedule and payments to customer Based on a contract and certain market data, gas and N2EX prices in the case of AMP, the customer is entitled to a certain payment. This payment is calculated by assuming a certain engine operating regime. This regime serves to calculate the operating profits and capacity payments. Contracts: - amp:

  • Run if operation appears profitable with a margin of 1 GBP based on the heren DA index price and the N2EX price.
  • 90% of the resulting profit are passed on to the customer, 10%
    are retained by DONG. The actual profit for DONG is given by the actual day ahead operation minus the share given to AMP
  • shaw:
    • Trade power on the N2EX as governed by the da schedule.
    • All profits are kept.
Parameters:
  • contract (str) – The type of contract.
  • list_of_contracts (bool) – If true does not calculate anything but prints a list of contracts encoded.
  • kwargs –
    • stochastic (pd.Series) or (float): With the index of self.data.data
      this pd.Series is added to the profit of da operations. Example: So a 1.96 std increase of the da cost is:
      -1.96*self.data.data[‘gas_da’].std()
calc_imbalance_revenue(method='amp', rev_split=0.5, equal_split=False, run_time=10)[source]¶

Calculate and share the imbalance revenue

Profits are devided according to the split, losses covered by DONG:

Parameters:
  • method (str) – Name of the method used to compute the imbalance revenue - ‘amp’: for a generator where imbalance is the deviation from the within day schedule. With generation cost. - ‘battery’: For battery or other asset, buying and selling only on the SIP, without generation cost.
  • rev_split (float) – Share of imbalance revenue retained by dong, must be between 0 and 1, defaults to 0.5.
